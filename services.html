What we offer

Services

Five ways we help a move go smoothly — pick one, or all of them.

01

House Removals

Full-service moves for flats, houses and everything in between. We handle furniture, appliances and fragile items with the same care whether it's a studio flat or a five-bedroom house.

  • Furniture dismantling and reassembly
  • Careful handling of fragile and valuable items
  • Flexible dates, including weekends
02

Office Removals

Relocating a business means keeping downtime to a minimum. We plan office moves around your working hours, so desks, equipment and files are ready to go the moment you need them.

  • Out-of-hours and weekend moves available
  • IT equipment and server handling
  • Labelled, floor-plan-matched unloading
03

Packing

A full packing service using proper boxes, wrap and padding — or we drop off materials in advance if you'd rather pack at your own pace.

  • Full or partial packing
  • Specialist packing for fragile and valuable items
  • Packing materials available separately
04

Storage

Short-term storage for the gap between moving out and moving in, or longer-term storage if you need somewhere to keep things for a while.

  • Flexible durations, short or long term
  • Inventoried and secure
  • Easy access when you need items back
05

Man & Van

A driver and a van for the smaller jobs — a single item of furniture, a few boxes, or a quick collection that doesn't need a full crew.

  • Booked by the hour or the job
  • Ideal for single items or small loads
  • Same-week availability where possible
